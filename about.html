About Karlee

    Karlee is an amateur artist continually working to develop her skill. She is your everyday lover of nature. All of her pieces of art are inspired by her outdoor adventures. Her father was a talented artist and fellow outdoor enthusiast and after his passing, creating art became her way to be close to him. She enjoys painting on wood, glass, canvas or anything else she can get her hands on. She uses mostly acrylic but occasionally uses oil, or water colors. Sketching was where she first developed her talent. Many of her earlier drawings and paintings are recreations of pieces that she greatly admires. Living in beautiful Utah she easily finds inspiration in the national parks, forests and deserts. If she’s not painting she’s either working as a registered nurse or frolicking through the dusty trails with her two adorable dogs and even cuter husband.
